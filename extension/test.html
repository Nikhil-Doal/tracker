Quick Tests

Use these buttons to generate test events:

Extension Status

Click "Check Extension Status" to verify installation

Expected Events

  • TAB_CREATED: When you open a new tab
  • TAB_ACTIVATED: When you switch to this tab
  • TAB_UPDATED: When you navigate to a different URL
  • TAB_REMOVED: When you close a tab
  • WINDOW_FOCUS_CHANGED: When you switch windows
  • IDLE_STATE_CHANGED: When you go idle (60s default)

How to Verify

  1. Open chrome://extensions/
  2. Find "Browser Telemetry Tracker"
  3. Click "service worker" link
  4. Watch console for [Telemetry Event] logs
  5. Click the extension icon to see stats
